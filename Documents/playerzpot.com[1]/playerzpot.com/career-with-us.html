Our Journey
Since our inception in 2015, we have consistently grown and evolved, transitioning from a business idea that shaped at a garage to becoming one of India's most popular gaming destinations. Read on to find out where we are and how we got here.
2022
2021
2020

2015
2015
Friends Yogesh Doiphode and Mitesh Gangar got together to translate their passion into the pursuit of excellence. They laid the foundation of EPL STAR Media Pvt with a vision to make gaming accessible more than just a hobby.
2016
EPL STAR entered the arena of Fantasy gaming with the launch of its very first web version by tapping on the pulse of the nation – Cricket! IPL made its fantasy gaming debut and was an instant hit among across India.
2017
Deeper insights and innovation coupled with countless improvisations led to the introduction of web version 2.0. Cricket and Kabbadi took centre stage.
2018
Quick to catch on to the mobile trend, the company defined its stronghold by launching EPL STAR Mobile Application with a massive fanbase of 2 lakh gamers.
2019
With competition getting tougher on one hand and gamer's unending quench seeking thrill and variety on the other hand, EPL STAR amped up its offerings by adding more games to the app.
2020
An eventful year through and through. EPL STAR’s popularity went skyrocketing to garner $3M -Series A funding. Cricketing Rockstars Smriti Mandhana and Bhuvaneshvar Kumar joined the family. Casual gaming made its foray on the EPL STAR platform with 5 games.
2021
The whole country was swaying to the beats of Rapper Kaam Bhaari and cheering for India ka Naya Maidan Campaign. Lo! And behold, the fan frenzy catapulted to over 7 million users.
2022
History in the making indeed. EPL STAR sways past the 1.5 Crore+ Trusted Users mark. There’s simply no stopping the trailblazer.
