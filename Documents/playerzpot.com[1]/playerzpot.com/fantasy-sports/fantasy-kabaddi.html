About Fantasy Kabaddi Game

The game of kabaddi is played with two teams of seven players facing off, the objective is to raid and defend. The raiders can eliminate the opposing team players and the defending team in their defense can eliminate the raider. The raider has a total of 30 seconds to complete the raid and thus collect a maximum number of raid points for the team. The game is believed to have originated in Tamil Nadu India, 4000 years ago. In 1936, the game of Kabaddi came into prominence and got international recognition after it featured as a demonstration sport at the Berlin Olympics. In 1990, Kabaddi became a part of the Asian Games in Beijing, China, and many international teams such as China, Japan, Malaysia, Iran, and South Korea started to compete on the world stage. The due recognition for the game of Kabaddi was further fulfilled when the Indian Kabaddi League was started in the year 2014. The Indian Kabaddi League has played an important role in Kabaddi's rising popularity in the country over the last 9 years.
Kabaddi can rightfully be described as the sport for every hard-working Indian with an attitude of never quitting. Being popularized on the international stage, it was only befitting that India should have its own fantasy kabaddi league. It did and what followed was the burst of the game on digital platforms. Fantasy Kabaddi quickly grew in popularity, and with fantasy kabaddi fans were able to engage well with the sport. Moreover, they were in it to win it from the numerous cash contests that were offered to them on fantasy gaming apps.

  • Get up-to-date information about kabbadi match preview and gain an in-depth understanding of the participating teams. Also, keep an eye out for the player's fitness levels.
  • Check the details about the venue and the weather report.
