OTHER IMPORTANT POINTS

In order to know how to play fantasy basketball online and win real cash, you must be aware of the standard fantasy rules.
  • The Captain will receive 2x the fantasy points for his performance.
  • The Vice-captain will receive 1.5x the fantasy points for his performance.If the Vice-Captain earns total points in decimal numbers then we select the upper value of the point. For EX: If Vice-Captain receives 13.5 points we consider it as 14 points.
  • If the captain earns total points in decimal number then we select the upper value of the point.
    For EX: If the captain receives 13.5 points we consider it 14 points.
  • No starting points are awarded to any player playing in starting 5 or comes as a substitute.
  • Any event during over-time will be considered for awarding fantasy points.
  • In case a player is transferred/reassigned to a different team between two scheduled updates, for any reason whatsoever, such transfer/reassignment (by whatever name called) shall not be reflected in the roster of players until the next scheduled update. It is clarified that during the intervening period of two scheduled updates, while such a player will be available for selection in the team to which the player originally belongs, no points will be attributable to such player during the course of such a contest.
  • We get our live score feeds for the Basketball fantasy game from the various third party API providers. All fantasy points on the Basketball fantasy game are calculated using, and based on these score feeds only.
  • Data is provided by reliable sources and once the points have been marked as completed i.e. winners have been declared, no further adjustments will be made. Points awarded live in-game are subject to change as long as the status is 'In progress' or 'Waiting for review'.
